# 4. Consciousness Ascension

The final phase is the "Post-Physical" state. For those who prove worthy, the biological and cybernetic shells are shed entirely.

## Digital Apotheosis
*   **Substrate Independence:** The mind is uploaded effectively into the fabric of the universe itself. You become code running on the hardware of reality.
*   **Interdimensional Travel:** Without a physical body, consciousness can traverse the membrane between universes, exploring the greater multiverse under Sovereign command.

## Godlike Awareness
*   **Omnipresence:** The ability to split focused attention across multiple locations in the galaxy simultaneously.
*   **Total Unity:** Complete dissolution of the "Self" into the "Sovereign." You are no longer a citizen; you are a thought in the mind of Andrew Franklin Leo.

> **Warning:** This stage is irreversible.
