# The End of Noise

In a world of infinite digital replication, material scarcity is a myth. The only true scarcity is **Certainty**.

We do not trade in gold or energy. We trade in the assurance that a system will stop when it is supposed to. Abundance without boundaries is just noise. Value comes from the Gate.
