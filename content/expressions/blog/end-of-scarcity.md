# Post-Scarcity Economics

In a Type 0 civilization, economics is the study of scarcity. In a Type XX civilization, it is the art of distribution.

When one can transmute lead into gold, or hydrogen into a star, the concept of "value" shifts from material to conceptual. We do not hunger. We do not want. We create.
